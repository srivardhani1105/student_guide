Course Details

Diploma in Civil Engineering

  • Department of Civil Engineering

Polytechnic (Three Years Course)

The Diploma of Engineering Studies aims to provide students with the ability to communicate effectively in English; a sound knowledge of basic engineering, scientific, computing and mathematical techniques; a knowledge of, and ability to apply, basic problem solving techniques; and basic knowledge and skills in one of the following fields: civil engineering; computer systems engineering; electrical and electronic engineering mechanical engineering.

Duration:
Polytechnic is a 3 year program approved by AICTE, Ministry of HRD Govt. of India & Affiliated to BTE Uttar Pradesh Board of Technical Education.

Specialization
Presently we offer the following specialization options:-

  • Computer Science
  • Electronics & Communication
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering (Production)
  • Mechanical Engineering (Automobile)
  • Civil Engineering

Admission Process & Eligibility for Polytechnic (courses)-2020-21

Affiliated To :-  Board Of Technical Education, UP. Lucknow

Course Duration :-  3 Years

Eligibility :- 10th pass with higher maths

Documents
Following documents are required for Registration / Admission.

  • Proof of Age
  • Matriculation Certificate
  • Intermediate Mark Sheet
  • Transfer/Migration Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Proof of Caste Certificate

Fees
Tution Fee -30000/- per year
(Exam, Library, Uniform and Personal Development are extra)
